WHY DAVE INFOMEDIA? WHAT MAKES US UNIQUE or DIFFERENT

We believe everyone has a moral duty to give back to the society that helped them grow and provided valuable resources. Dave Infomedia, an initiative of Dave technical Service (DTS) was founded with a deep commitment to serving the plastic industry by Mr Niranjan C Dave.

Since its inception, Dave Infomedia has always focused on sharing knowledge and helping the plastics industry grow. With aim to bridge the gap between expertise and aspiration, we publish technical handbooks that provide useful information for students, entrepreneurs and industry professionals, making learning easier and more accessible.

Dave Infomedia helps people learn and grow in the plastics industry through its books. It sparks curiosity, encourages new ideas, and provides useful knowledge for success. More than a publishing company, it is a platform for learning, a support for entrepreneurs, and a contributor for industry progress.

This website is developed with aim to create a global platform for information access, exchange and Dave Informdia stands as a beacon of knowledge, driven by a passion to transform education into action, ensuring plastics professionals and aspiring innovators have the tools they need to succeed and shape the future.

At Dave Informdia, we are committed to empowering people through knowledge-sharing and industry growth, focusing on the development of the plastics sector. We believe that by providing sustainable business solutions, especially to entrepreneurs and underserved communities, we can drive economic progress, rural development, and industry innovation.

Our technical handbooks serve as free knowledge platforms, equipping students, entrepreneurs, and professionals with essential insights to thrive in the plastics industry. Recognizing the importance of education and skill development, we actively contribute by conducting training programs, guest lectures, and mentorship sessions at leading institutions like CIPET, MCED, SISI, IIP, and SIES School of Packaging.

With an ever-growing demand for comprehensive industry knowledge, Dave Informdia has bridged the gap by publishing technical resources, creating an invaluable reference guide for institutions, businesses, and aspiring professionals. As an initiative of Dave Technical Services, Mumbai, we take pride in launching the "Packaging Guide"—a groundbreaking publication that serves the packaging community, fostering growth, innovation, and excellence. Through education, entrepreneurship support, and industry engagement, we aim to inspire, uplift, and drive meaningful change in the plastics sector.

Our Vision

Sharing Knowledge, Inspiring Growth, Connecting People

We strive to be the leading platform for technical knowledge and innovation in the plastics industry - connecting people, sharing expertise, and inspiring new ideas. Through information sharing, education and technical excellence, we wish to empower individuals with insights to create, grow, and contribute to a sustainable and thriving / blooming / flourishing (to keep any one word) plastics industry

  • Sustainability & Responsibility - Highlighting the commitment to eco-friendly practices and responsible innovation in the plastics industry.
  • Global Reach -Emphasizing the goal of creating a knowledge network that transcends borders and reaches professionals worldwide.
  • Industry Advancement -Positioning the platform as a driving force behind technical progress, new discoveries, and evolving industry trends.
  • Collaboration & Community -Showcasing how the knowledge hub fosters partnerships and connects experts, learners, and innovators.
  • Education for All -Reinforcing accessibility by making technical expertise available to beginners, professionals, and future leaders.
  • Cutting-Edge Insights -Communicating a commitment to staying at the forefront of the latest advancements in plastics technology and innovation.
  • Entrepreneurial Growth -Encouraging new ideas, startups, and business opportunities in the plastics sector through shared knowledge and resources.

Our Mission

Empowering Knowledge, Shaping the Future

We are dedicated to publishing high-quality technical handbooks that provide professionals, youths and entrepreneurs with the expertise they need to flourish in the plastics industry.

Through education, mentorship and hands-on skill development, we attempt to bridge gap between theoretical learning and practical application, turning knowledge into action. Our mission is to inspire progress, drive innovations, uplift communities and contribute to a responsible and thriving future for plastics.

  • Spread knowledge through insightful technical publications that educate, inform, and inspire industry professionals.
  • To empower youth and entrepreneurs with valuable knowledge, fostering careers in the plastics sector
  • To bridge the gap between learning and practical application, to innovate, create, and contribute to a sustainable industry growth.
  • Skill development through training, mentorship and educational initiatives.
